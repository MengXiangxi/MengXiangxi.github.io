Conference Abstracts Featuring UIH uEXPLORER PET Scanner

This is a webpage collecting conference abstracts related to the United Imaging Healthcare uEXPLORER total body PET scanner. The field of nuclear medicine and molecular imaging is evolving at an amazing speed, and the conference abstracts are often more up-to-date than peer-reviewed materials published in journals. Thus, we are summarizing inspiring abstracts concerning uEXPLORER.

A related repository is maintained to keep a record on peer-reviewed publications

2021 IEEE-NSS/MIC/RTSD Conference

This year's IEEE NSS/MIC/RTSD was held in online and based in Yokohama, Japan, from 16th to 23rd October. In this meeting, many programs are organized around total-body PET, including a session entitled "Total-body PET image reconstruction", and a session entitled "Emerging Applications and New Concepts in Medical Imaging". In convention, the conference proceedings will be published by IEEE, but it is not available right now.

Highlight

  • M-10-07 - Motion correction using multi-resolution scheme (MOTIONLESS) for 18F-FDG
  • M-05-065 - Design of the NeuroEXPLORER, a next-generation ultra-high performance human brain PET imager
  • M-05-201 - Regularization parameter selection in image reconstruction for short-duration
  • MO-03-05/M-06-330 - Sampling uEXPLORER Total-body PET Data to Mimic Altered Scanner Geometries
  • M-06-070 - Monte Carlo Simulations of Flux Rates with the NeuroEXPLORER Design Geometry based on Total-body Human Imaging Data
  • M-06-238 - Whole-body Patlak imaging with different axial FOV: a simulation study using uEXPLORER
  • M-16-280 - CNN-based time delay estimation in dynamic total-body PET kinetic modeling
  • WS-04-04/WS-04-07 - Total Body PET Reconstruction I/II
  • SC-07-05 - Total-body PET kinetic modeling and parametric imaging / Established and emerging clinical applications
