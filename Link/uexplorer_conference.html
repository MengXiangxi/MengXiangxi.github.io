Conference Abstracts Featuring UIH uEXPLORER PET Scanner

This is a webpage collecting conference abstracts related to the United Imaging Healthcare uEXPLORER total body PET scanner. The field of nuclear medicine and molecular imaging is evolving at an amazing speed, and the conference abstracts are often more up-to-date than peer-reviewed materials published in journals. Thus, we are summarizing inspiring abstracts concerning uEXPLORER.

A related repository is maintained to keep a record on peer-reviewed publications
