    Author Photo and Biography (IEEE style)

    Xiangxi Meng is a research assistant professor in the Department of Nuclear Medicine, Peking University Cancer Hospital & Institute. He received Biomedical Engineering PhD degrees from Peking University, Emory University, and Georgia Institute of Technology in 2019. His research interest is in the engineering aspects of molecular imaging, on the interdisciplinary frontiers of nuclear medicine and biomedical photonics.
